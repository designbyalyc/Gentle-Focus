Gentle Focus — Final UI

By Alicia Chow

Final UI Original Prototype GitHub Repository

Guiding Values

Gentle Focus is built around calm, clarity, and intentional focus. These values guided every decision — from the restrained colour palette to the single moving bubble. By focusing on simplicity rather than visual noise, the design encourages presence and mental stillness.

Feedback, Information Design, Mapping & Learnability

Feedback is intentionally ambient. The shifting colours represent the passage of time, while the bubble’s smooth movement confirms responsiveness. The mute/unmute icon provides clear mapping, and the entire interface is self-evident — users instantly understand how to interact without instruction.

Significant Changes Since the Previous Prototype

While the core concept remained the same, every detail was refined for stability and accessibility. Audio now starts muted with a proper toggle, motion was rebuilt using requestAnimationFrame, and the colour cycle runs on Date.now() for seamless pacing. Accessibility features and responsiveness were added to make the experience inclusive and industry-standard.

Refinement as Significance

The changes may appear subtle, but in calm-interaction design, refinement is significant. The Final UI focused on translating the prototype’s emotional intent into a fully resolved, technically stable product. The work demonstrates maturity through precision — smoother motion, balanced pacing, and restraint over embellishment — showing that subtle iteration can meaningfully transform experience quality.

Production Timeline & Process

The main production effort went into stabilising audio and motion across browsers. Debugging Safari’s autoplay policy required patience and problem-solving. Iterations on colour transitions and sound balancing reinforced the importance of controlled scope and user-centred refinement.

Aspect I’m Most Proud Of

The cohesive link between time, sound, and movement. The interface feels alive yet peaceful, and every part reinforces calmness through harmony.

Aspect I Found Most Challenging

Ensuring smooth, cross-browser audio without breaking immersion. Overcoming these technical issues strengthened both my design thinking and development confidence.
